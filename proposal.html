PokerWiki - Project Proposal

By Mateos Norian

Overview

I'd like to create a website that lists all professional poker players, and scrapes data about them (ie. total live winnings, where they went to school, things of that nature). Basically, Wikipedia for poker players.

Objective

Most of the poker wikis out there don't include much about the poker player's personal lives .. such as school, hometown, and I think that's the most interesting stuff about a player because it gives you some insight into their background. So, my objective would be to create a more personal poker wiki.

Audience

I think the answer to this question is fairly obvious, but my target audience would be enthusiastic poker players.. people who like to

  • Read about poker
  • Play consistently
  • Subscribe to poker training sites
  • Constantly looking to improve their game

Page content

Each wiki page would contain

  • Hometown
  • Favorite hand
  • Total live winnings
  • Links to scraped youtube videos of them playing
